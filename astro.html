Astrophotography

I am currently shooting with a Canon R8 paired with a 35mm f/1.4 and a 16mm f/2.8 lens. I first started using a Canon EOS M50 camera. This page will hopefully document my growth in Astrophotography. I use Sequator for stacking and Adobe Photoshop for further post-processing. I am currently only taking wide-field shots although I am interested in deep-field astrophotography as well.
