Japan 2024

I had the pleasure of going to Japan with some friends during a study abroad over the summer of 2024. We stayed in Tokyo for the majority of the trip, with some excursions to outlying towns (Nikko, Kamakura) as well as a weekend trip to Kyoto. While I wished that we were able to visit more areas in Japan, the experience, especially with friends, is unbeatable. I used my trusty old Canon G15 to take these pictures. My only regret was not using it more in urban environments (although the hustle and bustle of Japanese urban life would have made it cumbersome). Either way, I hope you enjoy these pictures which are some of the better ones from my photo album imo.
